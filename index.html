Privacy Policy — Twine

Last updated: 13 June 2026

Twine ("the app", "we", "us") is a private app for two people in a relationship to stay connected through a shared daily question and mood check-in. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By creating an account you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address and display name, used to create and identify your account. Authentication (including your password) is handled by Google Firebase Authentication; we never see or store your password ourselves.
  • Content you create — your answers to the daily question, your mood selections, and reactions you add. This content is shared with your linked partner, which is the core purpose of the app.
  • Pairing information — a one-time invite code and the link between you and your partner ("couple"), plus which platform (iOS or Android) you use.
  • Technical information — if you enable push notifications, a device push token so we can notify you (e.g. when your partner answers). The app does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

2. How we use your information

  • To create your account and sign you in.
  • To deliver the core experience — pairing you with your partner and syncing the daily question, answers, moods, and reactions between you.
  • To send you notifications you have opted into.
  • To keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

3. How your information is shared

  • With your partner — your answers, mood, reactions, and display name are visible to the one person you pair with. That is the intended function of the app.
  • With Google Firebase — we use Google's Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore to operate the app. Your data is stored and processed on Google Cloud infrastructure on our behalf. See Google's Firebase Privacy information.
  • We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with advertisers or other third parties beyond the service providers above.

4. Data storage & security

Data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore. Access is restricted by Firestore security rules so that only you and your paired partner can read your shared content. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest by Google Cloud.

5. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can ask us to delete your account and associated data at any time by emailing the address below; we will delete it within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it by law.

6. Children's privacy

Twine is intended for adults in a relationship and is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will remove it.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date on this page.
