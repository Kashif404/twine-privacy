Delete your Twine account & data

App: Twine (com.kashlabs.twine), by Kashlabs · Last updated: 13 June 2026

You can request deletion of your Twine account and all the data associated with it at any time. Follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to asadullahkashif108@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my Twine account".
  3. Send it from the same email address you use to sign in to Twine, so we can verify the request.

We will verify and permanently delete your account and associated data within 30 days of receiving your request, and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and profile (email address, display name).
  • Your daily-question answers, mood check-ins, and reactions.
  • Your pairing link to your partner and any invite codes you created.

What we keep

Nothing is retained after deletion, except where we are legally required to keep it. Routine backups are purged within 30 days, after which no copies remain.

Contact

Questions about deletion? Email asadullahkashif108@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
